| Features and System Manual|


  • Interactive Chart to display current available materials
  • Stock details like current available materials are maintained
  • Incoming transactions are done through purchases
  • Outgoing transactions are done through sales
  • Every transaction performed through the software is recorded
  • Bill is dynamically rendered and can be printed
  • Separate profiles are maintained for suppliers

| Operation Processes|


  • Sending Requsition to Supplier

    When you want to send a quotation to the supplier, go to the Purchase , click on new incoming stock, select the supplier from the dropdown list of suppliers, fill the form details and submit. Download the appearing Requisition form. Go to the supplier, suppliers list, select supplier, click on quotation Inquiry, Enter the message to be attached with the requisition form, add quotation from by uplaoding the one you just downloaded. Make sure the supplier selected when making the form is the same supplier receiving the quotation.

  • Sending Quotation to Buyer

    When you want to send a quotation to buyer, go to the Sales, Create and Order. fill the customer details and order details. Currently, the one MAIN material being selected from material list is the one whose quantity is deducted from the existing quantity. If the order consist of other materials, you need to view the material list in the Inventory section and deduct their quantities from there. Also, remember to deduct the quantity of the Accessories being used to make the order to keep everything up to date.

  • Recording Shop Products

    When you want to record a product in the shop, visit the Produscts Section in the system and fill the form for the product details. On issuing a product from the shop, remember to Update (icon) the quantity of the remaining products of the type issued.

    Only admins with staff batch can access the database and make changes. The changes include deleting an admin and/or adding
